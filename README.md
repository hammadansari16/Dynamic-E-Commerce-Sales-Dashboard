# Dynamic-E-Commerce-Sales-Dashboard

Dynamic E-Commerce Sales Dashboard 🛒📊

Tool: Microsoft Excel (Power Query, Pivot Tables, Advanced Charts)

📋 Project Overview

This project involved the creation of a comprehensive, end-to-end sales dashboard to monitor and analyze ₹5.97 Million in annual revenue from over 11,800 orders. The primary goal was to provide business leadership with a consolidated, interactive view of performance across various dimensions like geography, demographics, and sales channels.

🚀 Key Insights & Business Impact

High-Value Demographic: Discovered that Women customers drive ~95% of total sales (approx. ₹5.6M), enabling the marketing team to shift focus and optimize ad spend toward this high-conversion segment.

Peak Performance Trends: Identified May as the peak sales month (₹531k revenue), providing a roadmap for inventory planning and seasonal promotions.

Geographic Hotspots: Mapped regional sales to find that Maharashtra and Karnataka contribute over 25% of the total revenue, highlighting key areas for logistics optimization.

Channel Distribution: Analyzed multi-platform sales, revealing that Amazon (35.8%) and Myntra (23.5%) are the most effective sales channels.

Operational Health: Monitored order fulfillment to maintain a 90.6% delivery success rate, while identifying a 9.4% loss across cancellations and returns for process improvement.

🛠️ Technical Workflow

1. Data Cleaning & ETL
Data Volume: Processed a raw dataset of 31,000+ rows.

Integrity: Resolved inconsistencies in order status, currency formats, and product categories to ensure 100% data accuracy.

Automation: Used Excel functions and Power Query to standardize date formats and categorize customers into age groups (Teenager, Adult, Senior).

2. Data Modeling & Analysis
Pivot Tables: Aggregated large-scale data to calculate Monthly Sales, Gender-wise Revenue, and State-wise performance.

Advanced Formulas: Leveraged VLOOKUP, INDEX-MATCH, and Nested IF statements for dynamic data categorization.

3. Interactive Visualization
Dynamic Slicers: Integrated slicers for Months, States, and Channels, allowing stakeholders to filter the entire dashboard with a single click.

Visualization Suite: Designed a user-friendly interface featuring:

Gauge Charts for target tracking.

Stacked Bar Charts for gender and age-group comparisons.

Donut Charts for channel-wise order distribution.

Timelines for temporal sales analysis.
